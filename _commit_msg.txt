Floating orb — draggable anywhere, compact + expanded modes

The orb is no longer anchored to the bottom-center of the screen.
It's a freely-positionable floating assistant that you can drag
anywhere. It remembers its position between sessions.

Two modes:
  Compact  — just the orb (80x80), floats anywhere, always on top
  Expanded  — orb header + chat panel (440x600), glassmorphism

Interaction:
  Click the orb      → expand to chat panel
  Escape             → collapse back to just the orb
  Drag the orb       → move it anywhere on screen (Win32 native drag)
  Drag the header    → move the expanded window
  Win+J / "Hey Jarvis" → summon at saved position
